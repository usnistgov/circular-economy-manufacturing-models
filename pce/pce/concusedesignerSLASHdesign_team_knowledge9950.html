Use of Concept: designer/design team knowledge

Description

That information which a human design engineer brings to bear on a design problem, including design techniques, and implementation techniques. Many different types of design knowledge are used in different activities [2]

Examples:
Knowledge of design for X strategies:
- Design for manufacturing [13]
- Design for assembly [14]
- Design for the environment [15]
