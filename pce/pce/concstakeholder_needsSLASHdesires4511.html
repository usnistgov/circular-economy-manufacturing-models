Concept: stakeholder needs/desires

Description

The desires, needs, and preferences of stakeholders involved in the company and product lifecycle

Examples:
- Insights gained from targeted user research to expand to new customer segment (e.g., eco-conscious consumers)
- Expansion of a mobile phone product line to stay competitive in the market
