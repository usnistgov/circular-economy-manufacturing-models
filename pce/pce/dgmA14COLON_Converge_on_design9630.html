Model: A14: Converge on design

  • Activities
  • Concepts

Description

The model decomposition for design convergence consists of generating various design concepts, evaluating how well they fulfill requirements, and selecting a design concept to move forward with. In a circular economy, design convergence may be influenced by information about how specific design concepts will be used and/or handled at end-of-life.
