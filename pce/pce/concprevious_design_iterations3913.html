Concept: previous design iterations

Description

Information gained from prior iterations of the product

Examples:
- Previously released versions: How the product was produced; How the product was used; How the product was treated at end-of-life
- Previous "Design Change Requests" (from manufacturing): Information about requests from downstream engineering activities feeding back to design activities for changes to the part design [2]
