Use of Concept: market data

Description

Research to build a product/market research determination of a product opportunity [2]

Examples:
- Information about consumer behavior: Willingness to pay for circular products [4 - 6]; Knowledge about recycling, reuse, product circularity, etc. [6, 7]; Consumer preferences for product attributes, like form, function, sustainability, or circularity [8 - 10]
- Information about the competitive landscape: Competing products; Competing business models, including traditional and circular business models [11]; Branding/brand recognition [12]
- Market data on sales: Estimates of the total volume of the product which could be sold, its market life, and the production rate required to meet the projected market opportunity [2]
