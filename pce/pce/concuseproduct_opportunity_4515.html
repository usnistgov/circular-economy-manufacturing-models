Use of Concept: product opportunity

Description

The need that a business can fulfill with the product solution to bring value to consumers

Examples:
- An energy-efficient vehicle for mountainous regions
- Sorting for textiles from thrift store donations
- A long-lasting watch for ultra-marathon runners
