Concept: engineering specifications

Description

The actual values of the determined measurable criteria that the product meets

Examples:
- “The battery, the display, the SSO, the RAM, the trackpack shall be replaceable, with only commonly available tools and no additional calibration or testing tool” [59]
- A guaranteed 2 week repair turnaround for an e-bike
- A phone battery life of 14 hours
