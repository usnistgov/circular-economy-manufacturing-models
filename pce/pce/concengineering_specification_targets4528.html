Concept: engineering specification targets

Description

The goal or range for a set of determined measurable criteria that the product should meet to satisfy the determined customer needs

Examples:
- A quick repair turnaround of between 1 and 2 weeks for an e-bike
- A phone battery life of greater than 12 hours
