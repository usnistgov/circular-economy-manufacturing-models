Use of Concept: product design

Description

"Product Concept": Specification of a product in terms of customer requirements and relationship to existing products of the enterprise or its competition [2]

Examples:
- Product concepts can make prior solutions redundant by abandoning their function or by offering the same function with a radically different solution [22]
- Product concepts can involve reconsidering design and manufacturing decisions and putting multi-functional products on the market [22]
