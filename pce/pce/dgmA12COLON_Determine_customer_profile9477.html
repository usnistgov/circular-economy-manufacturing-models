Model: A12: Determine customer profile


Description

The model decomposition for determining the customer profile consists of gathering information from various stakeholders involved in both production and consumption, and using that information to establish the need that the product should fulfill. In a circular economy, these stakeholders might involve manufacturers, users, and service providers that handle end-of-life treatment such as recyclers or secondary marketplaces.
